Disc Golf in Sioux Rapids

By Co-Founder: Bonnie Meier

It is getting to be the time of year when people want to get outside and play disc golf. Use the Sioux Rapids map to help you find their disc golf course. The course features 9 holes that play around a small lake on the south side of town

To find the course on the Jubon Sioux Rapids map, click on the down arrow in the upper left corner (navigation box) of the map to scroll to the south side of the town. You will see the icon of a disc golf basket that indicates the start of the disc golf course. Click on the icon to find out more course information. We hope you have an opportunity to play this beautiful little course.
