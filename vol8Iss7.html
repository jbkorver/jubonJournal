Juliana Korver wins second US Open Overall Title
By Owner: Bonnie Meier with help from her daughter Juliana Korver

The US Open is a Frisbee overall event that is held every other year. This year it was contested in Santa Cruz, CA and Juliana Korver (daughter of the owner of Jubon Juweelen) successfully defended her title. The event overall winner is the one who gains the most points while competing in 7 different Frisbee events. Along the way, Juliana managed to break a world record in a timed event called MTA or Maximum Time Aloft. In this event, a person throws a disc into the air, typically into the wind, such that it catches the wind. The thrower must then cleanly catch the disc with one hand. Juliana's best throw was timed at 9.96 seconds which is now the new women's 45 and over record.


Juliana will next be competing in her first ever Freestyle Player's Association (FPA) World Championships in Brooklyn, NY the first week of August. Freestyle is an event judged on artistry, execution and difficulty. Juliana is very new to Freestyle and working very hard to learn this complicated discipline. We have freestyle discs available for sale at Jubon Juweelen if you want to give it a try yourself. To find more information about Freestyle, visit: www.freestyledisc.org
