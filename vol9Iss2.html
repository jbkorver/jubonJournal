Juliana Korver's Frisbee Adventures
By the owner's daughter: Juliana Korver

I have been asked to share a bit about my upcoming European Frisbee Events. Soon I will be leaving for Prague to compete in the 11th Annual Frisbeer Cup. This is a Freestyle tournament held in what I hear is a stunningly beautiful city that is also the capital of the Czech Republic. The event will take place indoors March 3-5. It has attracted a huge field of 80 competitors from all over the world who will be competing in up to 3 events. I will be playing in mixed doubles with my partner, Freddy Finner, from Germany. In women's pairs, I will be playing with another German Freestyler, Bianca Strunz who is currently the top ranked European woman in the FPA (Freestyle Player's Association). I have not played with either of these players before and will have only part of a day to put together a 3 minute routine with each of them. I expect I will learn a significant amount during the process. I will also be competing in open pairs with fellow American, training partner, and boyfriend, Rick LeBeau.

If you have interst in watcing the event, you can tune into the live stream March 3-5 at freestylefrisbee.cz/news/frisbeer-live-streaming/

After Prague, I will continue on to the Canary Islands for another Freestyle event the following weekend. The second event will be far less formal, though with a name like Frisbeer, I'm not sure if "formal" is the correct adjective to define the first event. The second event is a hat tournament that will take place on the beaches of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria. Everyone's name will go into a hat. Two names will be drawn from the hat and immediately those two players will begin their impromptu routine. As a beginner in this sport, I sometimes think that finding a partner can be the toughest part, so I am very intrigued and excited to play in a hat tournament where that anxiety has been removed.
