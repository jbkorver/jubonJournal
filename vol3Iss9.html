Find Treasure on Jubon

By Sales Representative: Teresa Brunsting

Now that school is in session, the Jubon treasure is back again. To claim this treasure you need to spend time on the Orange City map at the beginning of each month. Look at all the billboards on the map until you find one that sports a moneybag. When you find the special icon, go to the physical location in town for the store that showed the icon. In the store you need to look for the Jubon logo, the little red car that we call Jubon8. It will be about 6 inches by 4 inches and will be made of felt. It will be hidden somewhere in the store.

This month the treasure was located at Kraai's Furniture Carpet One and was discovered by Jordan Vermeer from Maurice, a Northwestern student, who noticed the moneybag located on Kraai's billboard. Once at Kraai's, Jordon received the following clues to help him locate the logo:
1. It is something that is in our Ad.
2. It is a place your Grandmother keeps her money.
3. We have three buildings and it is in the one that is a bright color.

Jordan found the Jubon 8 under a mattress in Kraai's Clearance Center and took it to the Chamber office on 8th Street and received Chamber bonus bucks for his efforts. Chamber bonus bucks can be spent just like real money at all Chamber member businesses.

A new treasure will be hidden on October 1st. Check all the billboards on Jubon to find the moneybag. Find Jubon8, return it to the Chamber office and receive Chamber bonus bucks.
