Alton Makes Tracks Online with Jubon

By Alton City Administrator: Dale Oltmans

Alton celebrated 125 years of making tracks in 2008. This small town still has a lot going on and has jumped on Jubon to make some online tracks as well. Alton is a fun place to visit and live. New shops are popping up and there are those tried and true businesses that everyone has enjoyed for years. Heading up the town is the Alton City Hall, which houses the City Administrator-Dale Oltmans, the Mayor-Ron Minten, and the City Council.

The City of Alton manages the Municipal Utilities and Parks and Recreation. They have many things to offer, including the band shell park, Roadside Park that includes a disc golf course, baseball & softball fields, swimming pool, a private 9-hole golf course, two schools, a public library and other community activities throughout the year. You don't get to be over 125 without working together through city, business, and community involvement. We welcome the City of Alton to Jubon.

Find Treasure on Jubon

By Sales Representative: Teresa Brunsting

The Jubon treasure was located at K-Products Outlet Store this month. It was found quite quickly by Paula Lourens. K-Products outlet store carries products made by Staples and has been in business in the downtown area for one year (they opened around the same time Jubon Juweelen did). They moved from at their original location on K-64 where they served customers for many years before that.

Paula took the treasure to the Chamber Office to receive her Chamber bonus bucks which can be spent in any store belonging to the Orange City Chamber. The clues this month were:

  1. Jubon 8 is afraid of the dark
  2. It is in a place that we store dishes in our homes
  3. What do golfer's say if they think they might hit someone with their ball?
The treasure was found in a dark cabinet in the front of the store. The fourth cabinet.

A new treasure will be hidden on November 1st. Check all the Orange City billboards on Jubon to find the moneybag to know where to begin your search. Be the first to find Jubon8, return it to the Chamber office and receive Chamber bonus bucks.

Jubon Juweelen Celebrates its First Birthday November 4th & 5th

By Co-Founder: Bonnie Meier

Jubon Juweelen, 107 3rd Street NE, in Orange City, Iowa will celebrate their first birthday on November 4 & 5. Everything in the store will be on sale except for our featured artist, Ron Netten's pottery. Pop a balloon to receive a discount on your purchase. Discounts range from between 10 - 50%.

In addition, there will be a new coupon on Jubon.biz every day of the week leading up to the birthday celebration. Juliana Korver, 5x Disc Golf World Champion, will be giving out freee minis with each sale of disc golf items. Join us and find great deals on holiday gifts, Christmas decorations & Sinterklaas collectibles as well as Dutch imports, antiques, fine arts, notions, beads, and books.

Wine Tasting at Woudstra's Meat Market

By Jubon user: Kerry Nieuwenhuis

Woudstra's Meat Market will be having their first wine tasting of the fall season on Saturday November 12th from 1:00 to 4:00 p.m.

Billie James, of the Little Swan Lake Winery of Estherville, will be there to showcase several of their wines, as well as entertain you with her voice and wit.

Stop by and sample some wine and see what new ideas she might have for the holiday season. Also, watch Jubon.biz for a special discount coupon for that day.
