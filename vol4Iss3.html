Linking back to Jubon

By Sales Representative, Teresa Brunsting

Jubon's goal is to make it easier for people to find businesses in small towns. The way that we are trying to do so is to create an online presence for both the businesses as well as the community. The stronger the presense is, the more everyone will benefit from it. In order for Jubon to succeed and thus for Jubon to help businesses compete with larger businesses and larger communities, we should all work together to increase our "voice".
