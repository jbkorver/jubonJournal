Letters From Ralph

Ralph J. Mouw, a native of Orange City, Iowa, never forgot his roots – even when he was halfway across the world during World War II.

In this collection of letters, you’ll find a firsthand account of what life was like during the war at home and overseas. Although letters were heavily censored during the war years, the editors have added comments to place them in context.

Pre Inventory Sale
By Owner: Bonnie Meier

On Monday, December 26, we will be open and all of our beads and findings will be 50% off. On Tuesday, our knitting supplies will be 50% off and on Wednesday our quilting items will be 50% off, including our Warm and Natural needled cotton. On Thursday all sewing items will be 50% off and on Friday discs, games and puzzles will be 50% off. Saturday we will feature Dutch food at 50% off, however, we will be closing at 3:13 that afternoon. This is our best sale of the year and is for the purpose of trying to get our inventory down a little before we start counting in January. All sales are for one day only. We will spend the month of January counting and getting our inventory recorded.


February - Shortened Hours
By Owner: Bonnie Meier

February last year was a very slow month for us. It is also the month that many people head south for at least a little while. Sharon Rens, our knitting/crocheting leader on Tuesday mornings will be heading south for the whole month. So after a little discussion we decided to curtail our 10:00 a.m. knitting group for the month of February. We have also decided to shorten our hours for February. Bonnie and Lee are hoping to go south for a couple of weeks, also, and Arlys and Idelle will both be spending several months away. So, for the month of February we will open at noon each day and will be closing soon after we have had our "coffee festival" – that’s what one of our granddaughters called our coffee time. However, when March comes along, we will be open regular hours again as we will need to start planning for the Tulip Festival, which will come sooner than we think.


Estate Sale in March
By Owner: Bonnie Meier

On March 20 we will have another Estate Sale. We already have several people signed up to bring some interesting items including some antique furniture. Our game plan is to open the back end of the store up, just for the estate sale, so that we have a little more room. We usually have up to 400 "new" items at our estate sales.
