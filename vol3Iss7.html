Alton has been Juboned!

By Co-Founder: Juliana Korver

 We are very happy to announce the addition of Alton to our online interactive, community map repertoire. Or as we say around Jubon headquarters, Alton's been "Juboned".

Last month we mentioned the possible addition of a new community, and Alton is it. Alton is a Sioux County town of about 1100 people located in the North West corner of Iowa. The community is located on the Floyd River and associates itself with the trains that travel the tracks that dissect the town.  

Currently we have 3 businesses on the Alton map: A-Town Liquor Store, L & M Gift & Apparel, and Salon Centra. In addition you can find the public library, post office, city park, Veteran's Memorial, fire station, a couple of schools and Roadside Park Disc Golf Course. If you "drive" to the Roadside Park, you find a billboard with pictures of the disc golf course as well as a link to the PDGA or the Professional Disc Golf Association where you can find the answers to nearly all of your disc golf questions.

We hope you enjoy your ride throug Alton and we hope you check back frequently as we work to add more businesses.

New Business on Jubon: A-Town Liquor Store

By Co-Founder: Bonnie Meier

On the 27th of June 2011, three brothers, Aric, Jamey and Jonathon Beltman took possession of A-Town Liquor Store in Alton, Iowa. This is the first joint business venture for the brothers. Aric manages the store while Jamey continues his career as a correctional officer and Jonathon as a truck driver.  The hours for their store are 12:00 noon - 6:30 p.m. on Monday through Wednesday, 12 noon - 8:00 p.m. Thursday and Friday and 11:00 a.m. - 6:30 p.m. on Saturday. Some of their new products include Pinnacle Whipped Vodka and Three Olive Dude Vodka. None of them had any retail experience before. Aric worked in a position controlling inventory for the last eight years. 

Look for A-Town Liquor Store on Alton's Jubon map at the main corner in Alton.

New Face of Jubon

By Co-Founder: Bonnie Meier

Teresa Brunsting is Jubon's new representative. She is a graduate of Northwest Missouri State University and has live in Orange City for almost 25 years. Teresa is married to Doug Brunsting originally from Hull, Iowa and has two children Cooper and Olivia.

Since moving to Orange City Teresa worked at K-Products for nearly a decade and then MOC-Floyd Valley School District for even longer.

Teresa will be hitting the streets to find more businesses and ultimately more communities to add to the Jubon family. When asked about her new endeavor, Teresa exclaimed, "I am excited about showing you what Jubon can do to promote your business and give you an online presence."
