Letters From Ralph

Ralph J. Mouw, a native of Orange City, Iowa, never forgot his roots – even when he was halfway across the world during World War II.

In this collection of letters, you’ll find a firsthand account of what life was like during the war at home and overseas. Although letters were heavily censored during the war years, the editors have added comments to place them in context.

UPS Thanksgiving Schedule
By Co-Owner: Bonnie Meier

UPS has issued their schedule for the Holiday season. Here is their Thanksgiving schedule. There will be normal pickup and delivery on Tuesday and Wednesday, November 24 and 25. All 2nd Day Air packages on Tuesday will be delivered by Friday, November 27 and Next Day Air packages picked up on Wednesday will be delivered on Friday, November 27; 2nd Day Air packages picked up on Wednesday will be delivered on Monday, November 30, except for those labeled for Saturday delivery. There will be no UPS services on Thanksgiving Day. Friday, November 27 will be normal pickup and delivery in most areas.


Call us at 712-737-8920 if you have questions.


Estate Sale Items 50% Off
By Co-Owner: Bonnie Meier

We had an estate sale starting September 9. We had many interesting, one-of-a-kind items including several collections. We were pleased to sell an entire Jan Hagara collection and many bells from a bell collection. One of the first persons to come to the sale was a man from Sioux City who collected brass. Not only were we able to accommodate him with many things from the estate sale, but we also had many other brass items in the store. If it was unique, he purchased it. We started with six tables full of items. We are down a few, but still have many items left. All estate sale items that are left are now 50% off. We will keep them around for a little while yet, but they will all be cleared out in time to celebrate our birthday when everything in the store is on sale.


Birthday Party November 5, 6, and 7

Vacation Rental in San Diego
By Co-Owner: Bonnie Meier

Co-Owner Juliana Korver purchased a house earlier this year in San Diego in the Point Loma/Ocean Beach area and is renting it out to tourists who visit the area. Lee and I had the opportunity to stay there from September 14 – 18. What a wonderful experience. The house is located only ten blocks from the beach. It is within walking distance and short driving distance from many of San Diego’s attractions. The house is lovely and easily accommodates seven people. All the dishes and furniture are brand new except for an old roll top desk that has been in the family since the early 1900s. If you were looking for a wonderful place to spend a vacation, I’d recommend that you look into this. The only suggestion I had for Juliana was that, since she is so tall, she needed a little step stool so that short people like me could reach the top shelves of the cupboards. Otherwise, everything was wonderful. You can get more information at http://www.vrbo.com/701588
